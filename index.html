Vodafone 5G Experience

Project Overview

Vodafone asked us to build an experience that would show the power of the Vodafone 5G network. We created an interactive door using real time rendering and face tracking to demonstrate their 5G implementations for retail, construction and manufacturing industries.

Guests picked one of the industries and when our door opened, a real presenter from the selected industry of the future started a real time conversation, explaining how operations in each industry will be improved with 5G connectivity. This "door into the future" concept effectively showcased what trends will become the new norm across different sectors.

The Experience works with a Camera that tracks the head position of the person in front of it. A custom camera rig within Unity translates the tracked head data into correct perspective rendering. Meanwhile a presenter is filmed on a green-screen set and the video is projected into Unity as a texture in realtime.

My Responsibilities

I was tasked with building and lighting the four different environments we used for this Experience.

  • Art Direction
  • Light setup
  • Interface animations
  • Building the environments
  • VFX

Technical Innovation

  • Real-time head tracking and face detection
  • Interactive door mechanism with industry selection
  • Custom Unity camera rig development
  • Live green-screen video integration
  • Perspective-corrected rendering
  • Multi-environment lighting for retail, construction, and manufacturing
  • Real-time presenter interaction system

Industry Applications

The experience demonstrated 5G connectivity improvements across three key sectors:

  • Retail: Enhanced customer experiences and operational efficiency
  • Construction: Real-time collaboration and remote monitoring capabilities
  • Manufacturing: IoT integration and automated production systems

Project Impact

Created an immersive 5G demonstration experience that effectively showcased future technology trends through innovative real-time rendering, interactive design, and live presenter conversations, providing guests with tangible examples of how 5G will transform their industries.

Google Interland Roadshow

Project Overview

Our work for the launch and roadshow of Google Interland, which aimed to help kids to be safe, confident explorers of the online world. Google Interland game was experienced by participants at Google's press conference as well as by children through kiosks set in different parts of Turkey.

Google Turkey asked us to produce a roadshow version of their Interland mini games that promote and teach internet safety for kids in a playful way. I was tasked to essentially recreate these games in a roadshow compatible version that operates on a three screen arcade machine with physical input options.

The games had to be shorter but still convey the same information and fun as the original web-based game. Additionally to redesigning the games, we also built an Interland theme ride and an additional mini game both in VR.

My Responsibilities

  • Game design and adaptation
  • Recreating all environments of the original game for streamlined experience
  • Animations and VFX development
  • Building and rendering the Interland VR Theme Ride
  • Designing and building a mini VR Escape Room game in the Interland Theme

Technical Challenges

  • Three-screen arcade machine integration
  • Physical input system development
  • Web-to-arcade game adaptation
  • VR theme ride implementation
  • Cross-platform compatibility for roadshow deployment
  • Child-friendly interface design

Educational Impact

The Interland roadshow successfully taught internet safety to children across Turkey through:

  • Interactive Learning: Hands-on gameplay that makes internet safety engaging
  • Immersive VR Experiences: Theme ride and escape room for deeper engagement
  • Accessibility: Physical arcade controls suitable for children of all ages
  • Wide Reach: Deployed across multiple locations throughout Turkey

Project Impact

Successfully adapted Google's web-based Interland games into an engaging, educational roadshow experience that reached thousands of children across Turkey, promoting internet safety through innovative game design, VR experiences, and interactive arcade technology.
